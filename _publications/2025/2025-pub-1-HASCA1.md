abstract: >-
  We proposes a classification framework based on handcrafted features, extracting 156 statistical, time-domain, and frequency-domain features from a total of 12 channels, including the tri-axial signals and their amplitudes of accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers.  The experimental results show that our approach achieves strong recognition performance across multiple device placements, achieving an accuracy rate of 71.4% and an F1 score of 71.8% on the verification dataset which confirms the effectiveness and efficiency of our method.
